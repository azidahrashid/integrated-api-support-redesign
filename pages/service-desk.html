Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is XT-FREEM and how does it work?

XT-FREEM is a comprehensive service desk platform designed to streamline your support operations. It provides ticket management, inquiry tracking, and a centralized hub for handling customer requests efficiently. Our platform uses advanced AI to categorize and route tickets automatically.
Account

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, enter your email address, create a secure password, and verify your email. You'll gain immediate access to all features of the platform.
Technical

What browsers are supported?

XT-FREEM supports all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser for the best experience and security.
Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.
Support

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our 24/7 customer support team through the "Create an Inquiry" button, live chat, email at support@xt-freem.com, or by phone at 1-800-XT-FREEM. We typically respond to inquiries within 2 hours.
Account

Can I change my email address?

Yes! Go to your account settings, select "Email Settings," and follow the verification process. You'll need to verify both your old and new email addresses for security purposes.
Technical

Is my data secure and encrypted?

We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Our platform is SOC 2 Type II compliant and undergoes regular security audits.
General

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. You can upgrade to a paid plan anytime during or after the trial period.
